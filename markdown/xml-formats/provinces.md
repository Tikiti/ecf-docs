## Overview

This is the complete Table III from the e-CF specification, containing all province and municipality codes used in:
- **Emisor**: `<Municipio>` / `<Provincia>`
- **Comprador**: `<MunicipioComprador>` / `<ProvinciaComprador>`
- **Entrega**: `<MunicipioEntrega>` / `<ProvinciaEntrega>`

> [!NOTE]
> Province codes are 6 digits (e.g., `010000`). Municipality codes are also 6 digits (e.g., `010100`).
> D.M. = Distrito Municipal (Municipal District)
