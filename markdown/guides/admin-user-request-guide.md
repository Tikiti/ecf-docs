## Overview

The **Usuario Administrador de e-CF** (e-CF Administrator User) is the physical person designated by the taxpayer to manage the identity of authorized signers and e-CF actions in the DGII Virtual Office.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> - Must be a **Physical Person** (Persona Física).
> - Must have a relationship with the taxpayer: Partner, Admin, Shareholder, Member, Fiduciary Manager, etc.
> - Must have their **own** Digital Certificate.
> - **Must be requested BEFORE** applying to be an Electronic Issuer.

---

## Requirements

1.  **RNC Relationship**: The person must be linked to the company RNC with one of the following authorized relationships:
    - Socio (Partner)
    - Socio Domiciliado en el Exterior (Foreign-domiciled Partner)
    - Administrador (Administrator)
    - Accionista (Shareholder)
    - Miembro (Member)
    - Gestor Fiduciario (Fiduciary Manager)
    - Socio Gestor (Managing Partner)
    
2.  **Digital Certificate**: Must possess a valid certificate for tax procedures from an authorized CA:
    - [Viafirma](https://www.viafirma.com/es/)
    - [Digifirma](https://digi.camarasantodomingo.do/)
    - [Novofirma](https://novofirma.com/)
    
3.  **Virtual Office**: Must have active access to the DGII OFV.

4.  **Identity Documentation**:
    - Dominican citizens: Valid cédula de identidad y electoral
    - Foreign citizens: Valid passport number

## Request Process

You can request this designation via **Presencial** (In-person) or **Virtual** channels.

### Option A: Virtual Office (Recommended)
1.  Log in to **Oficina Virtual DGII** with your credentials.
2.  Go to `Solicitudes` > `Actualización al RNC`.
3.  Complete the **Declaración Jurada** form for updating company data.
4.  Specify the person to be designated as **Usuario Administrador de e-CF**.
5.  Accept Terms & Conditions, then `Verificar y Continuar`.
6.  Attach required documents (letter, register) as a single PDF/JPG.
7.  Click `Procesar Cambios`.
8.  Save the **Request Number** for tracking.

### Option B: In-Person (Presencial)
Deposit the following at a **Centro de Asistencia Presencial (CAP)**:
1.  **Signed Letter**: Requesting the designation. For companies, must be on letterhead, signed, and stamped.
2.  **Mercantile Registry**: Copy of updated Registro Mercantil.
3.  **ID Copies**: If the person is not yet linked in RNC, provide documents to link them.

---

## Maintenance

- **Changing Admin**: If the admin changes:
  1. First, update the RNC data to exclude the outgoing admin
  2. Add the new admin relationship to the RNC
  3. Notify `facturacionelectronica@dgii.gov.do` with the cédula of both outgoing and incoming admin
  4. Wait for DGII to update the roles in the system
- **Validity**: The admin must keep their Digital Certificate valid to perform administrative tasks
- **Updates**: Keep the RNC relationship data current in the Virtual Office
